"When a ball dreams, it dreams it's a frisbee." - Stancil Johnson

Ultimate isn't widely considered a "sport" by many. A frisbee is seen as a children's toy, or to give your pets exercise. Frisbee is so much more than that, it's a lifestyle. And it IS a team sport, no matter what people say. It gets people together to work together towards a common goal. That's what sports are truly about, and that's what we believe in. So lets go have some fun.
